Why NestTrack exists

Built by an agent who lost his way chasing volume — and found it again.

Why I built NestTrack
Tom GaruccioFounder & producing agent · Cleveland
ThemA mountain of contacts you'll never talk to.
NestTrackThe people you actually talk to — and a system that makes sure none fall through the cracks.

I did what the industry told me to. Buy leads, fill the CRM, automate everything, chase volume. It didn't grow my business — it pulled me away from the part that actually did.

Here's what I'd forgotten: this business runs on relationships. High-quality conversations. A genuinely great client experience. Personally keeping in touch with the people who already know, like, and trust you. That's where conversion actually lives — always has.

So that's what NestTrack is built around. And the AI? It's there — working quietly in the background, running the process, the deadlines, and the paperwork, so your attention stays exactly where it belongs: on people.

Ads, cold leads, big-volume systems — they have their place for the right business at the right scale. But for most agents, growth was never another lead-hoarding machine. It's the relationships you already have, worked well. That's who NestTrack is for.
